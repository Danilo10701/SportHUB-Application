# **SportHUB: Level Up Your Game**
Ready to level up your fitness game? Finding the perfect place to work out has never been easier. SportHUB is your ultimate companion to discover top-rated sports facilities right in your neighborhood.
Whether you're looking for a local pitch, a swimming pool, or a hidden gem gym, we've got you covered.

# **Core Features**
SportHUB has four Core Features:
1) Your Personal Hub: A home screen designed to make you feel at home. Instantly access a curated list of the highest-rated facilities and discover the closest spots to your current location.
2) Interactive Map: Navigate your city with ease. Our intuitive map allows you to explore sports facilities geographically and dive into specific details with a single tap.
3) Advanced Explore Mode: Don't just search—discover. Filter by your favorite sport to find exactly what you need, complete with detailed info and honest user reviews.
4) Review Management: Keep track of your contributions. The dedicated Reviews screen lets you monitor, manage, or delete your feedback at any time.

# **A Personalized Experience**
Think that’s all? SportHUB takes it to the next level with Personalized Profiles. By logging in, you can unlock exclusive features to tailor the app to your needs:
1) Instant Favorites: Save your go-to spots directly to your Hub. Just click on a facility and hit the save icon to watch it "magically" appear in your favorites list for quick access.
2) Be Part of the Community: Share your experiences and help others! Once logged in, you can easily leave reviews and ratings for the places you’ve visited. Your voice helps the community stay active and informed.
